# Storyboard: Champagne & Strawberries (Winter Romanticism)

**Goal:** Create a high-end, immersive video that highlights the "Romantic Winter Date" USP of the farm.
**Vibe:** Cozy, Luxurious, Emotive, Warm (contrast between snowy exterior and cozy hospitality).
**Duration:** 15-30 seconds (optimized for Reels/TikTok).

- **Lighting:** Golden hour is essential. The contrast of the orange sun on white snow creates the "premium" feel.
